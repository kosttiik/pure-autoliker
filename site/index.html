In 4 steps

From install to first like in a couple of minutes

No sign-up and no servers. Everything runs locally, in your browser and under your own session.

1

Install the extension

Download the repo, turn on Developer mode and hit “Load unpacked”.

2

Open the pure.app feed

Log in to pure.app and open the profile feed.

3

Hit Start

Pick a filter and speed. For the first run, enable Dry-run to check it.

4

Likes run on their own

Close the popup — it keeps going in the background, counter shows on the icon.

Features

Everything you need for tidy automation

It likes smartly, remembers history and keeps a human rhythm — so everything looks natural.

Only new profiles

Skips already-liked ones, likes only new profiles and remembers them across runs (24 hours).

Human-like pace

Configurable delays between likes, a periodic long pause and a daily cap.

City & radius filter

From “Nearby” to “Whole universe” or a specific city — the extension sets the filter for you.

Speed presets

Calm, Normal, Turbo — or fine-tune delays and scrolling by hand.

Runs in background

Close the popup and switch tabs — liking continues, counter on the icon.

Dry-run mode

Highlights the hearts without clicking — a safe way to check the first run.

The interface

A tidy popup with everything at hand

Counters, status, location and speed on one screen. Fine-tuning and hints live just below.

Live counters

Likes this session, today and total — updated on the fly.

Trusted clicks (CDP)

Real input via the Chrome DevTools Protocol so Pure registers the likes.

Presets and fine-tuning

Quick modes plus manual delays, pauses and a daily cap.
